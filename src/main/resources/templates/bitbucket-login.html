  1. Go to bitbucket.org → your avatar (top right) → Personal settings.
  2. Under Access management, click App passwords.
  3. Click Create app password. Give it a label (e.g. api-explorer).
  4. Enable at minimum: Account → Read, Repositories → Read, Deployments → Read, Webhooks → Read.
  5. Copy the generated password — it will only be shown once.
  6. Return here and enter your Bitbucket email and that app password.
